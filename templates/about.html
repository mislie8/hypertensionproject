Hypertension prediction with artificial intelligence and machine learning

Millions of incidents of high blood pressure led to an enormous quantity of death or disability in the world, the leading cause is not clearly proved. With the help of artificial intelligence /Machine learning, this project will use certain factors age, weight height, gender etc. to predict whether a person is hypertensive and provide suggestions about the prediction results. Machine learning algorithms will be used to predict the outcomes depending on the data provided. Evidence that every single person uses an electronic device will contribute to the benefit of that system. This app will be design as a cross platform which mean it can be run on any operating system like that not only patient hypertensive has their health status monitoring but everyone else including youth. The result will justify the findings.
